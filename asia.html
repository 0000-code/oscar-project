Niseko Ski Resort

Niseko, located on the northern island of Hokkaido in Japan, is renowned as one of the premier ski destinations in the world. The area boasts a remarkable annual snowfall, with some parts receiving up to 15 meters of powdery snow, earning it the nickname "Powder Snow Capital."

Chengdu Xiling ski Resort

Xiling Snow Mountain Ski Resort, located near Chengdu, China, offers a thrilling winter sports experience. With diverse slopes for all levels, it's an ideal destination for skiing and snowboarding enthusiasts. Enjoy the breathtaking views and vibrant local culture alongside your winter adventure.

Jilin Vanke Songhua Lake ski resort

Vanke Songhua Lake Ski Resort in Jilin is a renowned 4A national scenic spot, just 15 kilometers from the city center. With 50 high-quality ski trails spanning 55 kilometers and a skiable area of 220 hectares, it can accommodate up to 15,000 skiers at a time. The resort boasts 5 FIS-certified trails and has been honored as China's Best Ski Resort at the World Ski Awards for eight consecutive years.
